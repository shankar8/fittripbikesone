MUTANT
There are purebreds and then there is this. A bike possessing the DNA of a MTB and a hybrid offered in a superlight package, thanks to the Alloy 6061 frame and Shimano 21 speed gearset to take any amount of elevation changes with the 4.0” tires lending it the capability to hit ANY/NO road. The mutant is India’s first ever bike to be offered with a military hydrographics paintjob. A breed apart.
