Marine is our version of a ‘friend with benefits’. The quintessential gentleman’s guide to fun for when he is feeling venturesome. What makes the bike a complete package is its perfect blend of fitness and adventure. When a hectic week calls for a little unwinding, a trip up the mountains seems like the perfect getaway. Marine is also the perfect excuse when working out at the gym becomes monotonous.
In our opinion, revitalizing for a long week ahead needs a little Marine and for the fitness conscious a little ride up the hill in the company of fresh air is the ultimate workout. The Marine is for the thrill seeker who can go to any limits just to get the chill down his spines. The Marine is for the boy who knows what exactly to do, to get what he wants.
