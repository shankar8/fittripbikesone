Duke

There are very few bikes out there which offer you the versatility that this bike offers. You will never have to think twice before you pedal whether you are going on road or off road. The seven speed shimano drivetrain offers enough ratios for those uphill and downhill battles, the alloy suspension makes any terrain easy to handle. Whatever your passion, pursuit or purpose the FitTrip Duke is here to make every moment an exhilarating one.
