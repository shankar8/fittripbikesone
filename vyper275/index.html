VYPER 27.5

It's rare to find a machine that matches the strength and agility of your muscle. We wouldn't call it anything short of a match made in heaven when you find a beast that compliments your move on the trail.

Presenting - Vyper. Designed and developed on the same ethos. Its strongly constructed frame can endure any grueling environment. It won't be surprising if you find yourself addicted to its sheer level of grip that comes from specially developed 2.5" WD small block tyres. The short wheelbase lets you flick the bike around corners with immense confidence.

The viper snake is known to decide how much venom to inject on the basis of its analysis of the situation. The Vyper has been built to replicate exactly these traits embodying what is synonymous with the leisure rider as well as the enthusiast.

Are you ready to be venomed?
